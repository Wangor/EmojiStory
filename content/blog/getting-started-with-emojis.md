# Getting Started with Emoji Storytelling

Emojis can convey emotion quickly. Begin by choosing characters like 😀 or 🐱 and place them in a setting 🌳.

Use arrows ➡️ to show action and end your tale with a surprise 🎉.

Happy storytelling!
